How to continue business as usual in these
unprecedented circumstances of Working From Home

To better emulate a workplace environment, designate a certain section of your home for use only for work purposes. Ensure that the area has adequate lighting, a plain background, comfortable seating and most importantly; a strong network connection. This mirroring will better reflect your workplace conditions thereby increasing focus and productivity.
